# Support Worker Profile Template — Design Brainstorm

## Approach 1: Midnight Luxury Accessibility
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Dark Luxury Accessibility — inspired by the reference image's deep navy and gold palette
**Core Principles:**
- Deep navy (#0a1628) backgrounds with warm gold (#c9a84c) accents
- Accessibility-first: large tap targets, high contrast, ARIA labels throughout
- Cinematic depth through layered backgrounds and glowing elements
- Warm human connection through photography and rounded organic shapes

**Color Philosophy:** Navy conveys trust, professionalism and calm. Gold signals warmth, value and aspiration. Together they create an inclusive premium feel that respects the dignity of both support workers and clients.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric card-based layout with a floating social post preview on the left and an editing panel on the right. The post preview is a 1:1 square mimicking Instagram/Facebook dimensions.

**Signature Elements:**
- Glowing gold ring around profile photo (matching reference)
- Flowing golden light-thread connectors between service icons
- Animated sparkle particles in the background

**Interaction Philosophy:** Tap-to-select service icons that glow brighter on selection. Inline text editing with a subtle gold underline. Video plays in a modal overlay with accessibility controls.

**Animation:** Entrance animations with staggered fade-in (80ms delay per element). Icon hover: scale(1.08) with gold glow pulse. Button press: scale(0.96) 160ms ease-out.

**Typography System:** Display: 'Playfair Display' (serif, italic for taglines). Body: 'DM Sans' (clean, accessible). Hierarchy: 48px display → 24px section → 16px body → 12px caption.
</text>

## Approach 2: Warm Earth Inclusion
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Warm Earth Minimalism — terracotta, sage green, and cream tones
**Core Principles:** Organic warmth, natural textures, gentle inclusivity
**Color Philosophy:** Earth tones feel grounding and human, reducing clinical coldness
**Layout Paradigm:** Single-column scrollable with full-bleed sections
**Signature Elements:** Leaf/organic shapes, hand-drawn style icons
**Interaction Philosophy:** Gentle bounce animations, soft hover states
**Animation:** Spring physics, 200ms ease-out
**Typography System:** 'Lora' serif + 'Source Sans Pro'

## Approach 3: Clinical Bright Accessibility
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Medical-grade clarity — white, teal, and accessible blue
**Core Principles:** Maximum legibility, clinical trust, WCAG AAA compliance
**Color Philosophy:** White backgrounds with high-contrast teal accents
**Layout Paradigm:** Grid-based, structured, form-like
**Signature Elements:** Bold borders, icon badges, progress indicators
**Interaction Philosophy:** Immediate feedback, no ambiguity
**Animation:** Minimal, functional only
**Typography System:** 'Atkinson Hyperlegible' + 'Roboto'
</text>

## CHOSEN APPROACH: Midnight Luxury Accessibility (Approach 1)

The deep navy and gold aesthetic from the reference image perfectly captures the premium, trustworthy, and warm feeling needed for a support worker profile. This approach will be implemented throughout the template.
