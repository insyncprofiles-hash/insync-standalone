## How to Use

### Step 1 — Edit Your Profile
Click the **Profile** tab in the editor panel on the right. Fill in your name, tagline, bio, location, contact details, and CTA button text. All changes update the post preview in real time.

### Step 2 — Choose Your Services
Click the **Services** tab. Toggle each service on or off using the switch. When a service is enabled, you can also customise its icon (emoji), label, and description. Selected services appear as glowing circles in your post.

### Step 3 — Add Your Photo
Click the circular profile photo area in the post preview. Select an image from your device. Your photo will appear immediately inside the gold-bordered frame.

### Step 4 — Review Your Caption
Click the **Caption** tab to see your auto-generated social media caption. Click **Copy Caption to Clipboard** to copy it, then paste directly into Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, or any other platform.

### Step 5 — Save Your Post
Click **Save Post** and use your browser's built-in screenshot tool to capture the post preview. On most devices: press `Ctrl+Shift+S` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+4` (Mac) to take a screenshot of the post area.

### Step 6 — Watch the Sample Video
Click the video thumbnail or the **MESSAGE TO BEGIN** button to play the 15-second sample video. This demonstrates how the template looks in a real support worker context.

## Accessibility Features

This template is built with accessibility and inclusion at its core:

- **WCAG 2.1 AA colour contrast** on all text elements
- **Full keyboard navigation** — every interactive element is reachable via Tab key
- **ARIA labels** on all buttons, toggles, and interactive elements
- **Screen reader compatible** semantic HTML structure
- **Reduced motion support** — animations are disabled for users who prefer reduced motion
- **Skip to main content** link for keyboard users
- **Alt text** on all images
- **Role attributes** on all interactive SVG elements
